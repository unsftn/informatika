Chair of Informatics is a unit at University of Novi Sad, Faculty of Technical Sciences. We provide a number of courses in computer science, software engineering and information technology at the University of Novi Sad, as well as life-long learning courses and training activities for the general public. We conduct research in cooperation with domestic and international partners and implement various development projects with industry partners.
